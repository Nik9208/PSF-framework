\section{Experiments}
The framework was validated across a suite of dynamical systems.

\subsection{Physical Representation Migration}
In the Lorenz and Rössler systems, we observed Representation Migration. As the prediction horizon increases, local coordinate predictability collapses, forcing the optimal representation to migrate to cross-variable or phase-based representations.

\subsection{Observer-Induced Migration}
In the Van der Pol oscillator, we tested observer-induced bias. Algorithms with distinct inductive biases (e.g., Random Forests) hallucinated structural transitions that were not physically present. Applying the ICCS metric filtered out these algorithmic hallucinations, demonstrating the robustness of the Observer Independence Principle.
