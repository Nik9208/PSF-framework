\section{Limitations}
The framework explicitly bounds its claims:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Predictive information accessibility is not strictly synonymous with physical causality.
    \item An optimal predictive representation does not guarantee the discovery of a fundamental physical mechanism.
    \item The framework is bounded by the completeness of the candidate representation space.
\end{itemize}
Future work, organized as Boundary Stress Tests, will explore hidden variables, causal/predictive divergence, and adversarial representation spaces.
